This Week on npm v20181116

This is the first in our weekly series on the most important publishes to npm this week. For now, we'll be posting the top 25 most downloaded packages that had new releases between Friday November 9 and Friday November 16, based on downloads in October 2018. Ready? Here's the list:

1) yargs@12.0.4: yargs the modern, pirate-themed, successor to optimist.

2) @types/node@10.12.4: TypeScript definitions for Node.js
