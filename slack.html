js.report

JSReport for Slack

Keep up with new releases to the npm packages you rely on from your Slack workspace.

JSReport posts to a Slack channel whenever an npm publish comes in for a package you're watching. Imagine a #releases channel that tells you when a new release of Lodash or Webpack happens in real-time.

Keep your team up to date with the npm packages they depend on. Don't get caught playing catch-up when a major security flaw like the ESLint hack surfaces. Get JSReport and be confident about what's in your ./node_modules.
